## ✨ Features

### 🛠️ Advanced Mod Management
Take full control over your game modifications. The built-in **Mod Manager** allows you to seamlessly install, enable, and disable mods without manually touching game files, ensuring a stable and customizable environment.

### 🎨 Dynamic Theming System
Personalize the look and feel of your launcher. Switch between different visual styles instantly:
- **Classic Theme:** A nostalgic, retro-inspired aesthetic.
- **Modern Theme:** A sleek, minimal, and contemporary interface.
- Support for custom XML-based theme definitions.

### 🎮 Integration & Social
- **Discord Rich Presence:** Show off what you're doing to your friends with real-time Discord status updates.
- **Community Service:** Stay connected with the community and access shared resources directly from the bootstrapper.

### ⚡ Power-User Tools
- **Global Hotkeys:** Use the launcher's features without leaving your game. Configure custom keyboard shortcuts that work system-wide.
- **Flag Manager:** Effortlessly manage FastFlags to optimize performance, unlock hidden features, or tweak the Roblox engine behavior.
- **Radial Menu:** A quick-access circular menu for the most important tools, allowing you to navigate the bootstrapper's functions with a flick of the wrist.

### 🎵 Built-in Media Experience
- **Integrated Music Player:** Enjoy your favorite tracks while you prepare for your next session, all within the launcher.
